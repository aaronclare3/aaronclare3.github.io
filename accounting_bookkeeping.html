Property Tax Return

Since all personal property taxes are state imposed, each jurisdiction may include different types of property in the tax assessment. Additionally, these taxes are often imposed by local governments, such as cities and counties, making the rules even less uniform across the country. Each state, or even each local jurisdiction such as a county, will have its own specific form you must fill out to report your personal property tax.

If you end up paying personal property taxes to your local government, the IRS allows you to claim a deduction for it on your federal tax return. However, the IRS requires you to satisfy certain requirements, regardless of how your government classifies the tax.

Clare Accounting Group will identify your personal property tax requirements and file the annual property tax return if deemed necessary to ensure that you or your business is able to claim a deduction for the property tax you have paid.

For an annual fee Clare Accounting Group can file your property tax return.


Sales Tax Return

State and local tax authorities are looking to maximize sales tax collections. We can help you understand and manage your sales tax obligations and prepare sales tax returns in an efficient and timely manner.

Types of Sale Tax Returns for Washington State:

  • Combined Excise Tax

This return is used for reporting your business income, sales tax, and use tax. Use this form if you do not qualify to use the tax returns below. The Combined Excise Tax can be filed, monthly, quarterly, or annually.

  • Business & Occupation Activities

This return is customized for annual taxpayers who report under certain B&O tax classifications and do not collect retail sales tax. Taxpayers can also report use tax on this form. The Business & Occupation Activities return is filed annually.

  • Retailing and Other Activities

This return is customized for annual taxpayers who collect retail sales tax and report under certain B&O tax classifications. The Retailing and Other Activities return is filed annually.

  • Sales Tax Remittance

This return is customized for annual taxpayers who gross less than $28,000 and owe less than $2,000 retail sales tax.


Clare Accounting Group will review your sales tax return requirements and will charge a monthly, quarterly, or annual flat fee based on the filing requirements of your business.
