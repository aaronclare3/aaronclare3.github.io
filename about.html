Meet the Team




Denise Clare, CPA

Denise has held a Certified Public Accountant license in Washington State since 1994. She has over 25 years of experience working with small, medium and large companies in various industries such as Construction, Wholesale Distribution, Retail, Service and Non-Profit. Denise is devoted to helping her clients succeed and meet all their compliance obligations as well as serving their bookkeeping, tax preparation, tax planning, payroll processing and financial accounting needs. She also enjoys working with individual tax clients, provinding tax planning and preparation services to maximize their after-tax income.

In her free time Denise enjoys cooking, biking, and spending time with her family.

Carissa Cooper, Director of Client Services

Carissa has a passion for helping others and finding the best solution for her clients' needs. She has 6 years of accounting experience. 5 of these years have been in public accounting where she prepared financials, sales tax returns, payroll tax returns, and individual and business income tax returns. She is originally from Boise, Idaho and has spent the last 8 years in Salt Lake City, Utah. Carissa dual majored in accounting and finance and received her Bachelors of Science degree from Westminster College. Upon receiving her undergraduate degree she attended the University of Utah where she received her Master's in Accounting with emphasis in taxation.

Carissa has a sound background in all entity types, QuickBooks, and accounting. She was raised with a phenomenal work ethic and pride in delivering on all she is asked to do. Additionally, she has a working understanding of financial markets, financial performance issues, and analytics of the same. Carissa has a strong moral fiber with keen interest and ability to operate within established time frames and under pressure. Her interests outside of work consist of skiing, white water rafting, cooking, and spending quality time with family. Here at Clare Accounting Group we are happy to welcome her to our team and look forward to her contributions.

Scott Clare, Client Relations and Business Development

Scott is most likely the first person you will talk to when you contact Clare Accounting Group. He is responsible for connecting you or your business to the Accounting and Tax professionals here at Clare Accounting Group. Scott will oversee the on-boarding process for our new clients.

With over 35 years of business experience and having founded and managed a Grocery Distribution Company in Western Washington, he has extensive knowledge in the area of business development. All our client’s businesses are unique, and he loves learning about each of them. His passion is to use his experience to help other small business owners with everything from their accounting needs to providing insights into improving operations and overall efficiency.

Some things he enjoys are family, skiing, fly fishing, sailboats and biking on the Burke-Gilman Trail…to the Fremont Brewery and other area breweries.

We hope you will call. Scott is sincerely looking forward to getting to know you and your business as well as introducing you to our firm.
