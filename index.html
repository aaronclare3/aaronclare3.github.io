IRS is Planning to Issue a New W-4 in 2020

Due to the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017 (TCJA) the IRS has updated the payroll tax withholding chart. In doing so the IRS will issue a new Form W-4 starting in 2020. This draft is not as simple as the old W-4 so please advise your employees to read the instructions carefully. If you have any questions for Clare Accounting Group regarding the new W-4 please let us know. Please see the IRS draft 2020 W-4 here
