The Cares Act

The Cares Act allowed many US taxpayers to receive a $1,200 stimulus check. A second round of stimulus allowed these same taxpayers to receive an additional stimulus payment totaling $600. If you should have received your payment but did not, you can file your 2020 Tax Return to receive your Recovery Rebate Credit.
