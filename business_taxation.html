Business Income Taxation

Clare Accounting Group can help you with your business income tax compliance annually. Our services include the following:

  • Preparation of your business income tax return(s) – Federal and State(s)
  • Electronic filing of your business income tax return(s) – Federal and State(s)
  • Review of financials for tax accounting adjustments to derive Taxable Income
  • Provide quarterly estimates if needed
  • Review Year End Tax Planning

Tax planning is a critical part of the growth of a business. If you are just starting a business, we can advise you on the different forms of ownership and the related tax consequences. It is important that you make the right decision. Clare Accounting Group can explain each choice and its implications. As your business grows and changes, the type of business entity you choose may need to change also.

If you have been operating your business for a while, we can provide year-end tax planning and evaluate the tax consequences of certain transactions.

An IRS audit can be an intimidating and complex process. If your business is facing an IRS audit, Clare Accounting Group can assist and guide you in dealing with tax matters and IRS audit procedures to ensure that you are properly represented when dealing with the IRS and other tax authorities.

Clare Accounting Group will review your financials and/or prior business income tax return to determine an agreed upon fee before completing business income tax returns.
